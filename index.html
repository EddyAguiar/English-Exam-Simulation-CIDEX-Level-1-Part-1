❓ Question 01: The man cooks the same thing every day.
✅ Answer: (B) FALSE
🟢 Explanation: He varies his meals and doesn’t cook the same thing daily.
🔵 Example: "I like variety," he says. "Some days I make pasta, others I make soup."
❓ Question 02: The man hates vegetables.
✅ Answer: (A) TRUE
🟢 Explanation: He mentions strongly disliking the taste of vegetables.
🔵 Example: He says, "I can't eat broccoli or spinach—disgusting!"
❓ Question 03: The man eats one apple, two bananas, and one orange every day.
✅ Answer: (B) FALSE
🟢 Explanation: His routine includes some fruit but not in that exact combination.
🔵 Example: He might say: “I eat an apple and sometimes grapes, not always bananas.”
❓ Question 04: Where is Marcel from?
✅ Answer: (C) He is from France.
🟢 Explanation: He introduces himself and says he is French.
🔵 Example: Marcel says, "Bonjour! I'm Marcel, from Paris, France."
❓ Question 05: What is the relationship between Marcel and Celine?
✅ Answer: (B) They are friends.
🟢 Explanation: In the audio, they greet each other and talk casually about life and interests.
🔵 Example: Celine says, "It’s great to see you again, my friend!"
